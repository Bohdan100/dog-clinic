About the clinik

The medical staff of our veterinary clinic - it's a team of qualified veterinarians with extensive practical experience. Our specialists adhere to the highest standards of veterinary care. You can safely entrust them with the care of your dog.

We have specialists in various fields: veterinary surgeon, sonographer, oncologist, urologist, gynecologist, dentist, ophthalmologist, dermatologist, etc. Our clinic is rightly called a multidisciplinary veterinary clinic. The staff of our clinic strives to restore the health of all patients and return the joy of communication, calmness and optimism to everyone who seeks help.
